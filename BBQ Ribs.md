#Dinner 
### Ingredients
- Ribs
- Seasoned Rub
- Vinegar
- BBQ Sauce
### Instructions
- Remove membrane from ribs
- Season ribs generously
- Pour vinegar onto the ribs
- Wrap in aluminum foil tightly
- Bake in the oven for 2.5 - 3 hours at 300Â°F
- Take the ribs out and glaze in BBQ sauce
- Broil until crispy
